Homework 2: Part 1: Matrix Representation
Find matrix representations respectively for three different kinds of data with at least two of them being different from the examples given in the class
● Describe the data, and
● Give matrix representation of the data, then
● Summarize mathematically how those matrices being used in the data analytical algorithm in each of the papers.

Part 2: Wedge Product
● Wikepedia to learn more about wedge product
● Choose one property of wedge product to prove it by yourself.
Homework 3: Part 1: Rotation Group SO(3)
● Understand SO(3) in depth by reading through the Wikepedia page , and
● Prove SO(3) is a manifold (RP3) and a group.
● It follows that SO(3) is a Lie Group (do not need to prove this). Read definition of Lie Group

Part 2: Lie Algebra
● Find lie algebra of SO(3).
● Find a basis of the lie algebra of SO(3)

Part 3: Curve
● Show that the helix α(t) = (acos t, asin t, bt) is a regular curve.
● Is it parametrized by arc length? If not, please reprametrize it so it will be parametrized by arc length.
● Find the curvature K(s) of the helix α(s).
Homework 4: Part 1: Surfaces
● Show that "almost" all quadratic surfaces are regular surfaces
● Show that sphere is a regular surface.

Part 2: Manifold
● Show that a regular n-dimensional surface is a manifold.

Homework 5: Part 1: Tangent Planes
● Prove that if a regular surface S meets a plane P in a single point p, then this plane coincides with the tangent plane of S at p.

Part 2: The First Fundamental Theorem and Riemannian Metric

● Show that A(R) is well-defined.
